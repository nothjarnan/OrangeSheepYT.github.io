Homework #1

Hi, my name is Jeff Jeffers! I am 20 years old. I have came here today to tell you about the footman’s job!
I started this job when I was 16 years old! Every day I have to wake up at 7am, t’was hard
at the start but you get settled in pretty fast!

The master is one rich man! He doesn’t know what to do with all his money! Every day I
have to wait the table for Mr.McGeogh-Bond and his wife. Also I have to polish boots for the master.

I started at garden hand but the butler saw me hard working so I got a promotion! I have to
clean cutlery and supply the fires with coal! I really enjoy my job. I have to pay my family to help
them get food on the table!

I come from a small village in Cookstown, I have 2 sisters and 1 brother, Alexander, Bell
and Matt. My mother is called Anne my dad is called Jeffery.
Home was hard work, to get food on the
table, it was extremely hard! My dad owns a farm and we get milk from cows, he was one hard
working fella!

At the Argory I never get holidays only the valet and lady’s maids gets to go
with Mr. McGeogh-Bond and Lady McGeogh-Bond. My salary is 15-29 pounds
a year. Money allows me to help home.
It’s a long day at the Argory I have work from 6am to 11pm!
I only get 7 hours of sleep although Im very used to it!

For sleep I have to sleep above the stables. The smell isn’t too pleasant indeed. The
valet and Lady’s Maids get there own room in the Argory, lucky them!

My best friend at the Argory is Matthew Stwert, He is a valet. I don’t really miss home because cause I know they’re getting fed.
I’m scared of the butler (he is bald) he’s a grump and gets angry easily.

LIFE AS A BUTLER

Hi, my name is Matt! I am the butler here at the Argory. I am going to be telling you about the
job butler! I am 40 years of age, and I have been working here
for almost 20 years! My salary is 50-60 pounds each year, I have
made around 1200 pounds from 20 years!

My duties in the dining room are to: Hire and dismiss staff, keys to the cellar, look after all the wine, iron the newspapers for the master,
discipline the staff, and keep scrap buckets and plate buckets in proper order!
I have to set up the pole screens so Lady McGeogh-Bond's Makeup doesnt run!
I also have to set the table for all the meals. I have to clean the cutlery
that is used to eat. I also tell the footmen to do stuff to make them work for their pay!
If people are at the front door I greet them! I pour brandy to male guests when they come!

I love my job as a Butler at the Argory and really enjoy the authority that comes with it.
